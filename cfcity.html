About The City

What is the Crypto Family City?
The Crypto Family City is a never ending series of projects by the Crypto Family linked all together to provide a city of easy to use and powerful crypto services throughout multiple smart contracts and multiple blockchains

What are the current plans of the City?
Currently the Family is focusing on assembling the first building of the City which is formed of multiple smart contracts and bridges in between contracts and blockchains such as Ethereum and Binance Smart Chain

Stages of building O

Stage 1

  • Release of FT (Family Token ) and FT staking on the Binance Smart Chain
  • Release of the Crypto Family Miner update 1.3.0 to that will help users stake their FT on the Binance Smart Chain
  • Release of FT on decentralized exchanges


Stage 2

  • Release of the Crypto Family advanced NFT smart contract on the Binance Smart Chain
  • Release of the Crypto Family decentralized NFT trading
  • Release of the Crypto Family Wallet that will help hold any token built on the Binance Smart Chain and interact with the advanced NFT smart contract, giving users the ability to create NFTs, hold NFTs, transfer NFTs, and trade NFTs


Stage 3

  • Release of the extension contracts that will bring more flexibility and features to both the FT token and the advanced NFTs smart contract


Stage 4

  • Release of the Crypto Family Bridge, making FT available and transferable between multiple and many blockchains such as Ethereum, Binnace Smart Chain, Tron and many others
  • Release of FT on centralized exchanges such as Binane
