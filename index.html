About Us

Crypto Family is an online community and family to everyone who wishes to join it. Our goals are many but are always focused on one concept... Growing Together.

We are here to help new people interested in crypto currencies learn and grow, whether their curiosity was technically related or software related or trading and investing related we are here for everyone. Crypto Family also runs a series of projects the most significant being the Crypto Family City

Frequently Asked Questions

Why are you doing all of this?

When the server first started its goal was to make a friendly, safe discord server who's purpose is helping people interested in bitcoin and crypto currencies to learn about cryptocurrencies, exchange ideas, tips, and experience.

Where you want to go?

As we progress and grow, our cause continues to grow with us. We do our best to be the change we want to see in the world of cryptos and in the real world by hard work and honesty and pure will to grow and evolve and build a great family that can be of help and example when it comes to everyone.

What are you focusing on?

Right now our focus is on the release of the Crypto Family City project and the release of the Crypto Family Token, projects we've been working on for the past year, and we're very excited to finally release for the community
