What Is The Crypto Family Miner ?
The Crypto Family Miner is a crypto currency mining tool made by the Crypto Family to simplify crypto mining and make it simpler and easier for anyone to mine. The miner also aims at improving performance and giving users easy control over their miners even while away from the miners

What Features Does The Miner Offer?
The Crypto Family Miner offers ease of use , improved performance, ease of control and management, giving it's users smooth powerfull mining and easy control and management over the mining machines Check 'How To Use' in the main menu for more info

Frequently Asked Questions

Is The Miner Gonna Support Any Other Cryptos?

Yes, future updates will bring more and more cryptos to the miner for everyone to mine

Does The Miner Charge Any Fees?

The miner charges a small developer fee of 1% that is then repayed back to the community through mining competitions and events. Stay updated with the Crypto Family socials to know of any upcoming events or competitions

Why Is It Detected As A Virus?

Mining softwares are detected as viruses by almost every anti-virus because hackers used to install these miners without the knowledge of the owner to use their devices for mining. But in this case you're the one installing and controlling the miner so you have nothing to worry about

How Much Will My Income Be?

Your income varies depending on your hardware, crypto your mining, pool you're mining in, and the up time of your miner

How Can I Join The Competitions?

When a mining competition or event is available it's details will be posted on our socials and on our website (Detials will incluse : How to join, rewards, how winners are selected, and time)

Why Can't I Click on 'Start Mining'?

The miner will not allow you to start mining if the inputed data is invalid, so please recheck the address you're using, the pool IP and the port
