About The Token

What is the Crypto Family Token (FT)?
FT is a token created by the Crypto Family to use in its projects such as the Crypto Family Bridge for governance and fees. Aside from that FT 2.0 was made to deliver one of the most secure and cheap tokens. (More about FT 2.0 coming soon)

FT 2.0 and the Crypto Family Bridge
FT is used to acheive PoVA 'Proof of Voting Authority' and pay fees on the Crypto Family Bridge to help secure the Crypto Family Bridge and all of it's transactions

FT 2.0 and the Crypto Family Vault
FT is the native currency to be used in the Crypto Family Vault project as payment method for both fees and services provided in the vault

What makes FT 2.0 special?
Besides being the native currency to be used in the Crypto Family projects, and the token used to secure the Crypto Family Bridge, FT comes with certain security and performance features never like before, that will enhance a users experience and assurance over their funds. (More details soon)

Features And Principles of FT 2.0

Staking

Unlike many other tokens and coins that are genrated through minting, new FT can only be found through staking of FT, making the FT community and FT holders the only users capable of finding new FT and placing it into existence, marking FT a token of its community and its community only
FT is also staked on the Crypto Family Bridge nodes to help ashieve PoSA 'Proof of Staked Authority' in between nodes and on the blockchains to help secure transactions and prevent any false transactions or attacks on the Bridge


Security

FT was built to help prevent orcastrated scams on the blockcahin with the use of smart contracts. As DEFI grows more and more smart contracts come to play on the blockcahin's ecosystem, and when a user needs to use these smart contracts he is requires to grant full allowance over his funds to these smart contracts, this poses a risk of funds being stolen by some malicious contracts. To prevent this from happening, a user has to go arround removing the allowance he gave to those contracts one by one. FT 2.0 offers the ability to revoke all allowances in one transaction, protecting users from malicious contracts and keep the power in users hands.


Performance

FT 2.0 is the first ever token to implement the use of assembly in it's code, making it one of the most effecient tokens on execution


Cheap Multiple Transfers

FT 2.0 comes with a built in multiple transfer function that can allow a user to send FT to thousands of addresses using one transaction. This helps ease transfers to multiple users and reduces the gas cost, in certain cases FT transactions have prooven to be the cheapest on the blockchain, even cheaper than those of regular coin transfers.

FT's Motive

While the world is full with cryptos of many purposes and technologies we felt that it still lacks a coin or token that can act as a native currency that can suport many transactions for cheap and provide a never ending life cycle of an anti inflationary token.

Satoshi Nakamoto's development of Bitcoin in 2009 has often been hailed as a radical development in money and currency, it introduced the world to the concept of decentralization and anti inflation. But while Bitcoin offered the world those features it had major issues that make it hard to rely on as an asset for simple payments such as buying a coke due to it's slow transactions and high fees, and even though Bitcoin is coded to have a limited amount of coins, it has a limited life cycle, when all Bitcoin are mined it will be very hard for new users to benefit from Bitcoin mining and it's gonna be hard for miners to keep up their mining operations off the transaction fees which will result in many miners shutting down their mining operations and eventually the end of the Bitcoin life cycle.

Advanced block chains such as the Binance Smart Chain, Polkadot and others offer a very fast and cheap payment method that can help offer cryptos fit for simple and big exchanges, however such blockchains lack the principle of anti inflation as their production of coins is never ending.

FT aims to combine the above principles and features to offer an anti inflationary token with very fast and cheap transactions, aswell as a life cycle that hardly ends, giving the world a token fit for holding and exchanging aswell as a never ending life cycle of staking and wealth redistribution.
