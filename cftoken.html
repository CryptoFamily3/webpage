About The Token

What is the Crypto Family Token (FT)?
FT is a token created by the Crypto Family to acheive the goal of a token that can be used as both a store of value and a quick cheap payment and exchange method

FT and the Crypto Family Bridge
FT is used to acheive PoSA 'Proof of Staked Authority' on the Crypto Family Bridge to help secure the Crypto Family Bridge and all of it's transactions

FT and the Crypto Family City
FT is the native currency to be used in the Crypto Family City project as payment method for both fees and services provided in the city

What makes FT special?
Besides being the native currency to be used in the Crypto Family City, and the token used to secure the Crypto Family Bridge, FT comes with certain features and principles that make it a true asset to rely on.

Features And Principles of FT

Staking

Unlike many other tokens and coins that are genrated through minting, new FT can only be found through staking of FT, making the FT community and FT holders the only users capable of finding new FT and placing it into existence, marking FT a token of its community and its community only


Anti Inflation

FT is an anti inflationary token with a fixed total supply of 1,000,000 FT and an APR of 4% from staking rewards
At release of the FT smart contract there will be a supply of 25,000 FT with the remaining 975,000 FT waiting to be found by stakers in the next 100-1000 years


Life Cycle

FT follows the Life Cycle principle which takes care of placing FT back into circulation after it's use in the Crypto Family City
When a user pays FT fees in the City, that FT is placed back into circulation rejoining the remaining FT in the line waiting to be found by the stakers
This feature makes sure that FT will have a never ending cycle of staking, minting, burning, and redistribution while keeping a total supply of only 1,000,000 FT


Cheap Multiple Transfers

FT is built on top of the Binance Smart Chain which allows for instant cheap transactions, FT also supports double, triple, quadruple and quintuple transfers to help ease transfers to multiple users and reduce the gas cost, in certain cases FT transactions have prooven to be even cheaper than BNB transactions on the Binance Smart Chain

Frequently Asked Questions

How to get FT

FT can be found for purchase on decentralized exchangesuch as Pancake Swap, it can also be traded p2p with other holders

How to get started staking FT

After you've got your hand on some FT you can use the CF miner or you can interact with the FT smart contract on BSCscan to stake your FT

What happens when all FT is staked out ?

If a day comes where all FT gets staked out stakers would still make FT from the fees paid in the Crypto Family City

Is there any other methods to earn FT rewards ?

FT can also be delegated to nodes on the Crypto Family Bridge, delegaters get a share of the fees paid by users on the Bridge
