About The Bridge

What is the Crypto Family Bridge ?
The Crypto Family Bridge is a protocol under development by the Crypto Family made to link blockchains such as Ethereum and Binance Smart Chain together along with other blockchains

How will the Bridge be run ?
The Bridge will be run and secured by nodes that will handle transactions from one blockchain to another (anyone can run a node). The nodes will use the Family Token to achieve PoSA 'Proof of Staked Authority' in between nodes and on the blockchains to help secure transactions and prevent any false transactions or attacks

What is the Bridge for ?
The Bridge provides inter blockchain communication, allowing tokens and coins to be transfered from one blockchain to another aswell as allow smart contracts on different blockchains to interact with one another

Will the Bridge have any transaction fees ?
The Bridge and its nodes will require a fee to run, aswell as to pay for the fees of the blockchains it's linking. The fees of the Bridge will be handeled in Family Token and are custom decided by the nodes through voting on every blockchain pair

Release Stages

Testing Phase I

The Crypto Family Bridge is set to full release in stage 4 of the Crypto Family City project, but there will be intensive testing by the developers before the full release. Testing Phase I will be a private testing phase available only for the developers and users with 100+ Family Tokens to participate in


Testing Phase II

After the developers are through with Testing Phase I, Testing Phase II will be available for users with 1+ Family Tokens to help expand the range of testing and help test the Bridge's performance on a wide range of many nodes


Testing Phase III

Following Testing Phase I and II the Bridge will become public on the Ethereum and Binance Smart Chain test networks for the testing of inter blockchain transactions, and performance of the nodes aswell as security and attacks protocols. During this phase anyone who wishes can perform transactions on the Bridge from one chain to another, however node running will remain exclusive to Family Token holders


Release

Succeeding testing the Crypto Family Bridge will go live on the Ethereum and Binance Smart Chain main networks, guides and documentations on running nodes and using the bridge will be released, Family Token will become available on both blockchains and transferable inbetween the two using the Bridge, the Bridge will also be open for any token or contract that wishes to use it

Frequently Asked Questions

When is the release date of every stage ?

The stages have no fixed release date and their release is linked to their development and testing

Requirements of running a node ?

Detailed requirements of running a Crypto Family Bridge Node will be listed after phase 2 of testing is done with, but for the moment the only known requirement is the need of a descent amount of Family Tokens to help achieve PoSA 'Proof of Staked Authority' on the node and securing the Bridge

Will the fees on the Bridge be expensive ?

Fees between blockchain pairs on the Bridge are decided by the nodes through voting, that fee usually varies depending on the gas price of the two blockchains, where the fee charged by the bridge should be enough to pay the nodes back for the gas they used transferring data between the two blockchains

Will the Bridge link blockchains other than Ethereum and BSC ?

The Crypto Family Bridge will link many and all possible blockchains, such as Tron, xDai, Matic, PolkaDot parachains and many others
