About The Bridge

What is the Crypto Family Bridge ?
The Crypto Family Bridge is a protocol under development by the Crypto Family made to link blockchains such as Ethereum and Binance Smart Chain together along with other blockchains

How will the Bridge be run ?
The Bridge will be run and secured by nodes that will handle transactions from one blockchain to another (anyone can run a node). The nodes will use the Family Token to acheive PoSA 'Proof of Staked Authority' in between nodes and on the blockchains to help secure transactions and prevent any false transactions or attacks

What is the Bridge for ?
The Bridge provides inter blockchain communication, allowing tokens and coins to be transfered from one blockchain to another aswell as allow smart contracts on different blockchains to interact with one another

Will the Bridge have any transaction fees ?
The Bridge and its nodes will require a fee to run, aswell as to pay for the fees of the blockchains it's linking. The fees of the Bridge will be handeled in Family Token and are custom decided by the users upon making their transfer

Development Stages

Stage I (Complete)

Development and testing of the smart contract, this stage is focued on developing and testing the features and missions of the bridge's smart contract, it will focus on security, voting mechanisms, punishments, gas efficiency, and interaction with transfered tokens smart contracts.


Stage II (complete)

Development and testing of the node's software, this stage is focused on developing a fast and light software that can help users easily join the bridge as a node, secure transactions on the bridge and prevent cheating, carry and forward transfer requests from one blockchain to another, forward token add requests, process and finalize requests, kick inactive nodes from the bridge,


Stage III (In progress)

Following stages I and II the Bridge will undergo heavy experiments to test security, scalability and flexibility, this stage will most likely be the longest as it is most essential to track down and fix any bugs or issues that might be present, as well as test and push scalability to it's best, to offer a finalized secure and effecient product to the crypto and blockchain community.


Release

Succeeding development testing the Crypto Family Bridge will go live on the following blockchains respectively, Ethereum and Binance Smart Chain, Ethereum and Polygon, Binance Smart Chain and Polygon. Guides and documentations on running nodes and using the bridge will be released, Family Token will become available on all three blockchains and transferable inbetween them using the Bridge, the Bridge will become open for any token or contract that wishes to use it

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the time line of every stage ?

The stages have no fixed time line, they're linked to their development and testing

Requirements of running a node ?

Detailed and final requirements of running a Crypto Family Bridge Node will be listed after release, but for the moment the only known requirement is the need of a descent amount of Family Tokens to help achieve PoSA 'Proof of Staked Authority' on the node and securing the Bridge, a stable wifi connection, and device (PC or server) to run the node.

Will the fees on the Bridge be expensive ?

The bridge is optimized for the cheapest of transactions, but doesn't come free of transaction fees. Transaction fees varies depending on the blockchain it's being transfered to.

Will the Bridge link blockchains other than Ethereum, BSC and Polygon ?

The Crypto Family Bridge will link many and all possible blockchains, such as Phantom, xDai, PolkaDot parachains (if proven possible) and many others
